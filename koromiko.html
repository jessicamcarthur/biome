Koromiko

Tea

Meet Kōromiko

Kōromiko (also known as hebe) is traditionally used in Māori medicine for digestive health. It is known to help treat vomiting and diarrhoea, helping to calm the gut and restore balance.

Why It's Good For You

Kōromiko contains natural compounds with anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties, which support the digestive system and help the body recover.

How To Make It

What You'll Need:

  • Handful of Kōromiko leaves
  • 1 cup water
    • xs

Let's Make It:

  1. Place the Kōromiko leaves in a pot with 1 cup of water and bring to boil.
  2. Simmer for 10–15 minutes until the water is a deep golden-brown colour.
  3. Strain the leaves and pour the tea into a cup.
  4. Drink in small doses: 1 tablespoon for adults, 1 teaspoon for children.
